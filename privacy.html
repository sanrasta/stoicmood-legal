Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Stoic Mood ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. If you sign in with Apple, we receive the information you choose to share (email, name).
  • Mood data: Mood entries, journal notes, daily challenge reflections, and engagement data (streaks, badges, levels) are stored locally on your device.
  • Notification preferences: Whether daily reminders are enabled and your preferred reminder time, stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription data: Purchase history and subscription status are managed by Apple and RevenueCat (see Third-Party Services below).

2. How We Store Your Data

  • On your device: Mood entries, journal notes, engagement data, and notification preferences are stored locally using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • In the cloud: Account authentication data (email, hashed credentials) is stored securely by Supabase. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Authentication and account management. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat may collect device identifiers and purchase-related data for app functionality. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Expo — Over-the-air app updates. May collect basic diagnostics. Expo Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above for the sole purpose of providing app functionality.

5. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use analytics SDKs or advertising trackers. We do not collect IDFA or use App Tracking Transparency.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data: Persists on your device until you delete the app or use the "Reset App Data" feature in Settings.
  • Account data: You can delete your account at any time from Settings > Account > Delete Account. This immediately and permanently removes your account and all associated data from our servers.
  • No email to support is required to delete your data.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. You can exercise all these rights directly within the app via Settings > Account. For additional requests, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Stoic Mood is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@stoicmood.app
